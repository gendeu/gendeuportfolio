👋 About Me

I’m Gen Deu Gagarin — a UI/UX Developer and Frontend Engineer with 7+ years of experience designing and building responsive, user-centric websites and enterprise web applications. I specialize in turning creative concepts into functional, pixel-perfect interfaces that enhance usability and elevate brand presence.

I’ve delivered 15+ production-level web solutions across e-commerce, SaaS, gaming, and sustainability industries. Recognized for improving performance and user experience, I consistently achieve faster load times and higher engagement across platforms.

Throughout my career, I’ve contributed to both corporate and consumer-facing platforms for companies such as Frontier Towers Associates Philippines, ADEC Innovations, Emids Healthcare, Emerson Electric Co., and Jade Entertainment. I’m experienced with WordPress, Wix, Bootstrap, HTML/CSS/JavaScript, and backend integrations using PHP, REST APIs, and MySQL.

I actively explore frameworks such as ReactJS, VueJS, and Laravel, applying design-driven development principles to ensure every product I build is both functional and visually engaging. My process is grounded in Agile/Scrum methodology and supported by design tools like Figma and Adobe Creative Suite.

Based in San Jose City, Nueva Ecija, Philippines, I’m always excited to collaborate on forward-thinking projects that blend creativity, usability, and technology.
